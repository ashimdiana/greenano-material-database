Reliability Score

How Reliability Score is calculated
$$ \begin{aligned} RS &= C_1^{x_{rs}} \cdot C_2^{y_{rs}} \cdot C_3^{z_{rs}} \\ x_{rs} + y_{rs} + z_{rs} &= 1 \end{aligned} $$
$C_1, C_2, C_3$ are the reliability coefficients for Temperature, Magnetization, and Coercivity respectively.
$x_{rs}, y_{rs}, z_{rs}$ are the weights for Temperature, Magnetization, and Coercivity respectively

How Performance Score is calculated
Each category is discretized into tiers $SF_T, SF_M, SF_C$. A tier score $PS_k$ is assigned based on thresholds.
$x_{ps}, y_{ps}, z_{ps}$ are the weights for Temperature, Magnetization, and Coercivity respectively.
$$ \begin{cases} PS &= PS_1^{x_{ps}} \cdot PS_2^{y_{ps}} \cdot PS_3^{z_{ps}} \\ x_{ps} + y_{ps} + z_{ps} = 1 \end{cases} $$

How Prevalence is computed
$$ p(E)=\frac{\sum_{m} AF_E(m)\,OS(m)}{\sum_{m} OS(m)} $$
$AF_E(m)$ is the atomic fraction of element $E$ in material $m$, and $OS(m)$ is its Overall Score.
